Pasta al Sugo

Description

The simple Italian pasta dishes are often the best.

Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 (400g) tin peeled tomatoes
  • 400g pasta
  • grated Parmesan cheese
  • Cayenne pepper

Steps

Prep:10min › Cook:40min › Ready in:50min

  1. Heat a oil in a pan over medium-low heat, add the onion and whole garlic and fry till yellow, about 4 minutes, turning often.
  2. Remove garlic. Add the carrot and salt.
  3. Add tomatoes and continue to cook over medium-low heat. Leave to cook, partially covered, for about 25 minutes, stirring frequently to prevent the sauce from sticking.
  4. Cook the pasta in salted water following packet instructions. Drain and toss with sauce, adding a little olive oil. Serve with Parmesan and a pinch of cayenne pepper.
